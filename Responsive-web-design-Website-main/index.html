About

My academic journey and life

  • 2001 - 2018

    Born and raised in Japan

    Growing up in Japan, a hub of technological innovation, I embarked on my journey in the digital world early on. At the age of 15, I delved into HTML and CSS, crafting my first website. This initial foray ignited my passion for technology, shaping my journey as a computer science.

  • 2019

    Univsersity in Malaysia

    Choosing to study abroad in Malaysia was a strategic move that not only enriched my academic journey but also polished my communication skills in a dynamic, multicultural setting. Immersed in diverse perspectives, I thrived, gaining invaluable experience that uniquely positions me to excel in global tech environments.

  • 2020

    Gap year in Japan

    In 2020, when COVID-19 became a challenge, I paused my studies. Yet, during that time, my interest in computer science flourished. I decided to prepare for a bachelor's degree in Computer Science, which I pursued in Budapest, Hungary.

  • 2021 - current

    Computer Science in Hungary

    During my university years, I have been mastering many programming languages. I honed my skills further through a front-end developer internship and actively engaged in hackathons and tech events to expand my professional network.
